Ingredients:
 - 1 portion Spaghetti
 - 1 egg
 - 1/2 cup Whatever vegetable/ham/sausage/rose(shredded meat)
 - 1 teaspoon Soy sauce
 - 1/2 teaspoon Lima Been Sauce from Pi county
 - 1/4 teaspoon Thirteen Spice
 - 1/4 teaspoon Salt
Directions:
  1. Cook the spaghetti till 'Al dente' or 'Molto al dente'
  2. Fry as Chinese-style fried noodle.
